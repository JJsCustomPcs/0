About JJ

Hey, I’m JJ. I’ve been passionate about building and fixing PCs since elementary school. I’ve built systems ranging from budget-friendly gaming rigs to powerful workstations. I love making tech approachable and fun, and I’m planning to study hardware engineering with the dream of working at industry-leading tech companies.

My mission with JJ's Custom PCs is to help people get the perfect PC without the stress. Whether you need a budget build, a maxed-out gaming machine, or just help assembling your parts, I’ve got you covered.

I focus on precision, performance, and aesthetics, making sure every build is not just functional, but also visually pleasing. Your PC will run great and look great at the same time.
